OUR MISSION

The Silicon Hills Project is bringing Austin's young builders under one roof. Our first house, the Summit House, is coming to West Campus at UT Austin.

The idea is simple: bring ambitious students and founders together and give them a place where they can meet, work, and build side by side. Good desks, stocked fridges, fast internet, and doors that stay open 24/7, a place they want to return to every day.

The house is only the setting. What matters is the people inside it. UT Austin's young talent is scattered across campus, West Campus apartments, office buildings, and coffee shops that close at 10:00.

When ambitious people work next to each other every day, ideas move faster, teams form naturally, and projects turn into real companies. The first house is just the start. If it works, we grow more houses, then founder houses, where teams live and build together, a network where Austin's youngest talent can build and stay.
