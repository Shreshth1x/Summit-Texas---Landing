OUR MISSION

Silicon Hills Project is bringing Austin's builders under one roof. Our first house, Summit House, is coming to West Campus.

The idea is simple. Bring ambitious students and founders together and give them a place where they can meet, work, and build side by side. Good desks, quiet rooms, fast internet, and doors that stay open late. A place they want to return to every day.

The house is only the setting. What matters is the people inside it. Austin's talent is scattered across campuses, apartments, offices, and coffee shops that close at ten. When ambitious people work next to each other every day, ideas move faster, teams form naturally, and projects turn into real companies.

The first house is the start. If it works, we grow: more houses, then founder houses where teams live and build together. A network where Austin's talent can build and stay.
